# ErbilVerse – UI Content Copy (For Interface Mockups Only)

This document contains the text content to be used inside the ErbilVerse website and application interface mockups. The goal of these screens is to visually present the platform concept. The tone must be clean. Avoid financial or investment language.

## 1) Landing Screen
- **Title:** ErbilVerse
- **Tagline:** The Digital Layer of Real Estate
- **Short Description:** A living digital city where property, projects, and market activity come together.
- **Main Button:** Enter the City

## 2) City Map Screen
- **Section Title:** Explore the Digital City
- **Description:** Navigate Erbil in an interactive environment and discover real districts and developments across the city.

## 3) Property / Building Information Screen
- **Title:** Property Overview
- **Fields (labels only):**
  - Location
  - Total Area
  - Digital Market Value
  - Project Information
- **Button:** View Details

## 4) Digital Market Screen
- **Title:** Live Digital Market
- **Description:** Market values change dynamically based on real platform activity.
- **Subsections:**
  - Latest Transactions
  - Recent Activity
  - Market Movement

## 5) Developer Screen
- **Title:** For Developers
- **Description:** A new digital presence inside Erbil’s interactive city layer.
- **Key Points:**
  - Showcase projects in 3D
  - Connect with new audiences
  - Receive direct engagement
  - Strengthen digital positioning

## 6) Transparency & Security Screen
- **Title:** Transparency Built In
- **Points:**
  - Verified Users
  - Real-Time Activity Log
  - Clear Ownership Records
  - Secure Digital Environment

## General Design Notes
- Focus on clarity and confidence.
- The platform must appear operational, not conceptual.
- The purpose of the UI is to present ErbilVerse as a digital urban platform and marketplace environment.
